CampusConnect – The Ultimate College Companion (RIT Social Media App)
CampusConnect is a next-generation college community platform meticulously crafted to empower student life at Rajiv Gandhi Institute of Technology (RIT). With an intuitive design and an array of intelligent features, the app is built to foster engagement, collaboration, and productivity among students, making it the digital heartbeat of the campus.

🚀 Key Features & Highlights
🌐 Dynamic Campus Feed
Stay in the loop with real-time updates on college life. Whether it’s official announcements, trending posts, event promotions, or club news—CampusConnect keeps the campus connected and buzzing.

📅 Event Hub & College Calendar Integration
Browse upcoming events by category: Technical, Cultural, Sports, and more. Sync directly with the college's academic calendar for a seamless planning experience.

💬 Real-Time Communication
Chat one-on-one or in groups, share media, and even jump into high-quality video calls. Stay in touch with friends, classmates, or project teams—all in one place.

🕹️ Multiplayer Games & Leaderboards
Break the academic routine with fun, multiplayer mini-games. Compete on daily and weekly leaderboards to earn bragging rights and exclusive rewards.

📚 Learning Hub
Access curated study materials, lecture notes, flashcards, and quizzes. Use the built-in study planner and academic calendar to stay on track and motivated with a points-based reward system.

🕵️ Anonymous Confession Wall
Share your thoughts freely and anonymously. Vent, confess, or inspire—without revealing your identity.

🪪 Digital Student ID
No more fumbling through your wallet. Carry your official RIT Student ID digitally, accessible anytime via the app.

🛒 Campus Marketplace
Buy, sell, or exchange books, electronics, notes, and other student essentials. A secure and trusted space just for RIT students.

🍽️ Cafeteria Menu & Pre-Booking
Know what’s on the menu in advance and pre-book meals to avoid queues and save time during busy hours.

🚌 Bus Tracking System
Track college buses in real-time using GPS. Get arrival alerts and plan your commute with confidence.

🧳 Lost & Found Board
Report lost items or browse listed items that have been found by others. Helping students recover what matters.

🛠️ In-App Complaint Box
Submit grievances, suggestions, or facility-related issues directly through the app. Ensuring student voices are heard.

👥 Peer Collaboration Rooms
Join virtual group study rooms, prep for interviews, or collaborate on projects with dedicated spaces for focused interaction.

🔍 Why CampusConnect?
CampusConnect isn’t just an app—it’s a complete ecosystem for student life. Whether you’re attending a tech fest, studying for finals, or just looking to unwind, Kampus Connect is your all-in-one solution to a smarter, more connected campus experience.

Welcome to the future of campus engagement—Kampus Connect, where your college life goes beyond classrooms.
